2026 Pricing Model Evolution
Hybridization Trend
Agentic software is reverting to per-seat models but with new definitions of "seat"
Moving away from pure pay-per-action or pure usage-based
Hybrid approaches combine:
Base platform fee
Per-seat component
Usage overages or overage tiers
SaaS Management Shift
SaaS costs increasingly driven by AI features, hybrid pricing, and usage-based models
Creates spend volatility throughout contract lifecycle
Traditional annual budgeting models becoming less reliable
Need for dynamic cost allocation and monitoring

Pricing Strategy Implications
For Service Providers
Value-based pricing emerging as customers focus on ROI rather than usage
Shared-risk models becoming more common (success-based pricing)
Transparent cost breakdowns increasingly required by enterprises
Hidden costs in monitoring, data storage, and compliance underestimated
For Buyers
Evaluate true TCO (not just API costs)
Negotiate SLAs and performance guarantees
Assess lock-in risk with vendor-specific agents
Plan for AI feature cost inflation as models advance
