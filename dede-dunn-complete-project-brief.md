## FIXTURE STYLE TIERS & PRICING

All mushroom/bollard fixtures (Zones C, E) must follow traditional Cape Cod aesthetic.

### OPTION 1 — Budget-Friendly ($35–60/fixture)
- **Style:** Modern minimalist bollard
- **Material:** Aluminum/poly composite
- **Finish:** Black or textured bronze
- **Height:** 12–15"
- **Output:** 3–4W LED, 150–200 lumens
- **Issue:** NOT Cape Cod appropriate; aesthetic mismatch
- **Examples:** Harbor Breeze (Lowe's), Sunforce, generic aluminum

### OPTION 2 — Traditional Style ⭐ PRIMARY RECOMMENDATION ($90–150/fixture)
- **Style:** Classic lantern or decorative bollard
- **Material:** Cast aluminum or brass body
- **Finish:** Oil-rubbed bronze, aged copper, antique brass
- **Height:** 15–20"
- **Output:** 5–7W LED, 250–350 lumens, 2700K
- **Pros:** Authentic period look, quality construction, works with Cape Cod
- **Examples:** 
  - VOLT® Lighting traditional bollard series
  - Hinkley Lighting Cape May or Nantucket collection
  - Landscape Forms traditional bollard
  - Restoration Hardware coastal pathway lights
  - Kichler traditional path light series

### OPTION 3 — Premium Heritage ($180–300+/fixture)
- **Style:** Ornate heritage lantern or coach lamp
- **Material:** Heavy cast brass or powder-coated steel
- **Finish:** Aged brass, burnished copper, oil-rubbed bronze
- **Height:** 18–24"
- **Output:** 5–10W LED, 300–500 lumens, 2700K
- **Pros:** Museum-quality detail, heirloom durability
- **Cons:** Expensive for full pathway; best for statement entry pieces
- **Examples:**
  - Visual Comfort & Co. heritage collection
  - Restoration Hardware vintage coach lamps
  - Circa Lighting coastal lanterns
  - Beveled Glass traditional pathway lanterns

---

## RECOMMENDED FIXTURE STRATEGY

**Hybrid Approach:** Mix Option 2 (traditional) with Option 3 (premium) for visual impact without full premium cost.

- **Entry focal points (high-visibility):** 2–3 premium fixtures (Option 3) flanking entry or at driveway turn
- **Pathway fill (visual continuity):** 4–6 traditional fixtures (Option 2) along walkway and planting beds
- **Total mushroom/bollard count (Zones C+E combined):** 6–8 fixtures
- **Estimated budget for all path/bollard lights:** $840–$1,200

**Rationale:** Premium pieces make a statement at the entry. Traditional fixtures create elegant pathway continuity without premium cost on every unit. Looks intentional, not budget-constrained.
