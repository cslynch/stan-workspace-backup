## OPTION 1: COZUMEL, MEXICO üá≤üáΩ
**Why:** Best value, amazing snorkeling, closest to Texas

### Flight Costs
- **San Antonio (SAT) ‚Üí Cozumel (CZM):** ~$300-450/person roundtrip
- **Total for 2:** ~$600-900

### Accommodation (7 nights, April 24-May 1)

#### AIRBNB OPTION
- **Budget Airbnb:** $30-50/night = **$210-350 total**
- **Mid-range Airbnb (beachfront):** $80-120/night = **$560-840 total**
- **Luxury Airbnb (private pool, ocean view):** $150-200/night = **$1,050-1,400 total**

**Pros:**
- Way cheaper
- Full kitchen (save on meals)
- More authentic local experience
- Flexibility

**Cons:**
- No staff/concierge
- You handle everything yourself
- May need to arrange own snorkel tours

#### RESORT OPTION
- **Budget resort (all-inclusive):** $150-200/night = **$1,050-1,400 total**
- **Mid-range resort (all-inclusive):** $250-350/night = **$1,750-2,450 total**
- **Luxury resort (all-inclusive):** $400-600/night = **$2,800-4,200 total**

**Pros:**
- All food/drinks included
- Beach gear provided
- Tours/activities arranged
- Zero planning needed

**Cons:**
- More expensive
- Less authentic experience
- Often away from town

### Activities & Food
- **Snorkeling tours:** $50-80/person (half-day), $80-120 (full-day)
- **Meals (if Airbnb):** $15-30/person per meal
- **Rental car (optional):** $30-50/day = $210-350 total
- **Scooter rental:** $20-30/day = $140-210 total

### TOTAL COST ESTIMATE (Cozumel)
**Budget (Airbnb + street food):** ~$1,800-2,500  
**Mid-range (nice Airbnb + dining out):** ~$3,000-4,000  
**All-inclusive resort (mid-range):** ~$4,000-5,500

**Why:** BEST beaches & snorkeling in Caribbean, softest sand, clearest water

### Flight Costs
- **San Antonio ‚Üí Providenciales (PLS):** ~$450-700/person roundtrip (1-2 stops)
- **Total for 2:** ~$900-1,400

### Accommodation (7 nights, April 24-May 1)

#### AIRBNB/VILLA OPTION
- **Budget inland villa:** $380/night = **$2,660 total**
- **Mid-range beachfront villa:** $800-1,200/night = **$5,600-8,400 total**
- **Luxury beachfront villa:** $1,500-2,500/night = **$10,500-17,500 total**

**Pros:**
- More space
- Full kitchen
- Private pool (usually)
- Better value for groups

**Cons:**
- Groceries are EXPENSIVE in T&C
- Need rental car
- You plan everything

#### RESORT OPTION
- **Inland resort (Kokomo):** $380-490/night = **$2,660-3,430 total**
- **Beachfront luxury resort (Seven Stars):** $655-1,600/night = **$4,585-11,200 total**
- **All-inclusive (Beaches Resort):** $7,300-11,700 for 7 nights (2 adults)

**Pros:**
- Beach access
- Water sports gear included
- Restaurants on-site
- No grocery shopping needed

**Cons:**
- Very expensive
- Food/drinks still costly (except all-inclusive)

### Other Costs
- **Rental car:** $50-150/day = $350-1,050 total (+ 27% taxes!)
- **Snorkeling tour (shared):** $125/person
- **Private charter (half-day):** $1,250-1,700
- **Meals (if not all-inclusive):** $25-50/person per meal
- **Groceries:** VERY expensive (see research notes)

### TOTAL COST ESTIMATE (Turks & Caicos)
**Budget (inland resort, cook some meals):** ~$6,500-8,000  
**Mid-range (beachfront resort, dining out):** ~$10,000-14,000

**NOTE:** Turks & Caicos is BEAUTIFUL but expensive. Everything costs more ‚Äî food, activities, rental cars, etc.

## MY RECOMMENDATION

### BEST OVERALL VALUE: COZUMEL
**Stay:** Mid-range beachfront Airbnb ($80-120/night)  
**Why:**
- Amazing snorkeling (seriously world-class)
- Clear turquoise water, soft sand
- Save $2,000+ vs Turks & Caicos
- Close to Texas (short flight)
- Great food scene in town
- Can do resort-style excursions without resort prices

**Estimated Total:** $3,000-4,000 for the week

**Sample Itinerary:**
- Day 1: Arrive, settle in, beach time
- Day 2: Snorkeling tour to Palancar Reef
- Day 3: Beach day, explore San Miguel town
- Day 4: Snorkeling + beach
- Day 5: Day trip or spa day
- Day 6: Beach, sunset dinner
- Day 7: Relax, pack

### BEST IF MONEY ISN'T AN ISSUE: TURKS & CAICOS
**Stay:** All-inclusive at Beaches Resort OR beachfront villa  
**Why:**
- THE softest sand and clearest water anywhere
- Incredible beaches (Grace Bay is legendary)
- Amazing snorkeling
- Very safe, English-speaking
- True tropical paradise

**Estimated Total:** $10,000-13,000 for the week
