## High-Impact Use Cases

### Financial Services & Back-Office

**Automated Loan Processing**
- NLP-powered automation of loan processing workflows
- Eliminated manual review bottleneck
- Result: Faster approval cycles, 40% reduction in processing time
- Compliance: AI + human oversight maintains accuracy and regulatory alignment

**KYC (Know Your Customer) Automation**
- NLP-powered extraction and verification
- AI flags anomalies for human review (HITL model)
- Result: 99.5% accuracy, 40% faster documentation processing
- Compliance: Full audit trail maintained

**Claims Handling**
- AI routes claims by complexity and risk profile
- Simple claims: AI handles end-to-end
- Complex claims: AI prepares summary for human adjuster
- Result: 15,000+ employee hours saved per month (from one case)

### Operational Process Automation

**Manufacturing & Supply Chain**
- **Predictive maintenance:** AI forecasts equipment failures, prevents downtime
- **Order fulfillment:** AI optimizes logistics and reduces waste
- **Compliance:** Automated document processing with 99.5% accuracy

**Dresser Natural Gas Solutions Case Study**
- Challenge: Legacy code-heavy logbooks, low adoption
- Solution: AI-powered process automation platform (FlowForma)
- Result: Replaced legacy system, automated approval workflows, unlocked adoption
- Impact: Faster processing, better compliance tracking

### Customer-Facing Applications

**Sales Enablement**
- AI sales assistant: Reviews customer data, suggests next actions
- Smart recommendations: Based on engagement history and deal stage
- Results from case studies:
  - 30-50% CSAT improvements
  - Measurable sales uplift
  - 70% cost reductions in some scenarios

**Customer Service Chatbots**
- **Lyft case study:** Claude-powered chatbot (Amazon Bedrock)
  - Handles routine inquiries autonomously
  - Escalates complex issues to human specialists
  - Result: 87% reduction in average resolution time
  - HITL model enabled efficiency + quality

**Product Personalization**
- Amazon product recommendation system example
- AI analyzes: browsing history, purchase history, cart contents
- Result: Increased cross-selling and upselling, improved conversion rates

### HR & Learning

**Employee Learning Path Optimization**
- AI recommends learning paths based on skills data and career goals
- Aligns training with internal capability frameworks
- Tracks learning ROI and completion
- Reduces training waste and improves capability development

**Hiring Process Automation**
- High-volume, administratively heavy work (ideal for AI)
- Directly tied to customer experience (frontline staffing)
- AI screens resumes, schedules interviews, conducts initial assessments
- HR teams positioned to demonstrate clear financial ROI

## Strategic Implementation Patterns

### The "AI Studio" Model (PwC)
- **Centralized hub** bringing together:
  - Reusable tech components
  - Frameworks for assessing use cases
  - Sandbox for testing
  - Deployment protocols
  - Skilled people
- **Outcome:** Links business goals to AI capabilities, surfaces high-ROI opportunities

### Start Small, Scale Strategically
- **First agent:** Single, low-complexity use case
- **Measure everything:** Time saved, accuracy, cost per transaction, customer impact
- **Iterate:** Refine before adding complexity
- **Most reliable path to ROI:** Starting narrow, scaling only when proven

### Make or Buy Decision Framework
1. **Build internally:** Custom solution, high upfront cost, long time-to-value
2. **Buy ready-made:** Faster deployment, lower cost, less customization
3. **Partner with AI consultant:** Balance of speed, customization, support

**2026 Trend:** Most successful deployments use combination of all three
